Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026 · Applies to all Four Alba LLC apps: ScanSitPlay, BLAPL, Too Easy QR, Poker Clock, and MTT GTO Poker Trainer. Draft — review before publishing.

Overview

Four Alba LLC ("we", "us") builds mobile applications. This policy describes what information our apps collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. We design our apps to collect as little data as possible.

Information we collect

Depending on the app and the features you use, we may collect:

  • Information you provide — such as a name or email address if you create an account, join a league, or contact support.
  • Device and usage data — such as device model, OS version, crash logs, and anonymized analytics used to improve app stability.
  • Camera access — apps with scanning features (ScanSitPlay, BLAPL, Too Easy QR) use the camera solely to read QR codes. Images are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted.

How we use information

We use collected information to operate app features, maintain accounts and league memberships, fix bugs, and respond to support requests. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers required to run our apps (such as cloud hosting and crash reporting), and only as needed. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention & deletion

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see our account deletion instructions.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Email support@fouralba.com.

Account & Data Deletion

Applies to all Four Alba LLC apps.

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data from any Four Alba app at any time. There are two ways:

1. In the app

If the app has an account, open Profile → Settings → Delete account. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

2. By email

Email support@fouralba.com with the subject "Account deletion request". Include the app name and the email address associated with your account. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted

Your account, profile information, and app activity tied to your account. Anonymized analytics that cannot be linked to you may be retained. Records we are legally required to keep (if any) are retained only as long as required.
